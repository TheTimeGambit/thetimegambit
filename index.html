The project

The Time Gambit is a Chess themed 2D puzzle platformer created withing the scope of the course DH2650 Game Design at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. The course ran for 8 weeks during which time this demo was designed and implemented from scratch by a team of 5 students.

The Story

The Time Gambit follows the story of lone plucky pawn sent out on a quest through the enemies castle to ascend to queenhood and save it’s side from ruin. Blocking the path are various puzzles, requiring cunning to overcome. Aiding in the quest is the hourglass of time, allowing brief visits to the past clearing the way forward in the present.
